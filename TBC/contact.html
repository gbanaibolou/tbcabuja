Trinity Baptist Church Abuja has been designed to reflect and recapture gospel-centered worship. We see worship as an act of offering sacrifice and praise to God. This is our priority: to worship God according to how He commands. Central to our worship is the proclamation of the Word of God by way of biblical exposition. Trinity stands in the tradition of Reformation faith and practice.
