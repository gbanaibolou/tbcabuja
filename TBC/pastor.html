Pastor's Profile

My name is Abutu Peter Joshua. As far as theological education and preparation for pastoral ministry is concerned: I am a graduate of European Missionary Fellowship (EMF) and The London Seminary (LS). I was a trainee pastor both at Highbury Baptist Church, London and Kabwata Baptist Church, Zambia. I have been in pastoral ministry for eleven years, and married to Joy with the blessing of four children.

My roots are in the Reformed Baptist Christian conviction. I grew up in a CMML/CEFN (a Plymouth Brethren Ministry) and did serve there in different capacities.

Currently, I am a missionary pastor of Kabwata Baptist Church Zambia, leading a new Church plant in the city of Abuja. My desire is to see a biblically sound, Christ-centred, God-honouring Church taking root in the city of Abuja and to seek the replication of the same across Nigeria.
